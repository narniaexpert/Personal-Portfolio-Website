When I was three years old my mother saw me standing alone in the living room. After a short wait I moved forward a few feet and stopped. When she asked me what I was doing I said, "I’m waiting in line at Disneyland." From a young age I have had a lifelong passion for Disney theme parks and have always aspired to be an Imagineer. My goal is to use technology and creativity to develop innovative experiences for Disney theme parks in Asia.
